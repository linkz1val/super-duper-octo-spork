About

A young voice writing

Parinita is a Class 12 student, NEET aspirant, and the author of Words to My Future Daughter — a quiet letter to the women who will come after us.

Between textbooks and storyboards, she writes about mothers, daughters, silence, and the small rebellions that shape a life.
